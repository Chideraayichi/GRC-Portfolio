## Projects

### 1. Information Security Risk Assessment — Healthcare
**Organization:** Crestview Regional Medical Center *(Fictional)*
**Framework:** NIST CSF 2.0 / HIPAA Security Rule
**File:** `Risk - Assessment - Healthcare - Crestview.docx.`

A full information security risk assessment was conducted for a fictional regional medical center with 250,000+ active patients and 2,800 staff. Performed in alignment with the HIPAA Security Rule mandate under 45 CFR 164.308(a)(1)(ii)(A).

**Includes:**
- Regulatory and compliance context (HIPAA, HITECH, NIST CSF 2.0)
- Asset inventory with ePHI classification
- Threat identification matrix with HIPAA impact analysis
- Risk register with 16 identified risks (5 Critical, 5 High, 4 Medium, 2 Low)
- Every risk is mapped to a specific HIPAA Security Rule citation
- Prioritized remediation roadmap with owners and timelines
- CISO-ready executive summary and compliance conclusion

### 2. Third-Party Vendor Risk Assessment — Healthcare
**Organization:** Crestview Regional Medical Center *(Fictional)*
**Vendor:** MedCloud Solutions LLC *(Fictional)*
**Framework:** HIPAA/HITECH, NIST SP 800-161
**File:** `Vendor - Risk - Assessment - MedCloud - Crestview.docx.`

A formal vendor risk assessment was initiated as a remediation action for a Critical risk identified in the Crestview healthcare risk assessment, demonstrating end-to-end GRC workflow from risk identification to control implementation.

**Includes:**
- Vendor tiering framework (Tier 1 Critical — direct ePHI access)
- Full vendor profile including BAA status, certifications, and subcontractor mapping
- 6-domain security questionnaire (23 control areas) covering:
  - HIPAA Compliance & Data Governance
  - Access Control & Identity Management
  - Data Protection & Encryption
  - Vulnerability Management & Patching
  - Incident Response & Business Continuity
  - Physical Security & Workforce Controls
- 6 formal findings with risk ratings, HIPAA citations, and remediation deadlines
- Overall vendor risk scorecard by domain
- Formal Conditional Approval recommendation with contractual escalation criteria

### 3. Information Security Risk Assessment — Legal / Law Firm
**Organization:** Harrington & Wells LLP *(Fictional)*
**Framework:** NIST CSF 2.0
**File:** `Risk - Assessment - LawFirm - Harrington - Wells.docx.`

An end-to-end information security risk assessment for a fictional 350-attorney global law firm, addressing risks unique to the legal industry, including attorney-client privilege, document management system security, and insider threat.

**Includes:**
- Asset inventory across legal-specific systems (iManage DMS, M365, Active Directory)
- Threat identification matrix including BEC, ransomware, data exfiltration, and insider threat
- Risk register with 9 identified risks (4 Critical, 3 High, 2 Medium)
- Remediation roadmap with timelines, owners, and NIST CSF alignment
- Professional executive summary and conclusion
